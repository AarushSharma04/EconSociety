Curriculum

To promote financial literacy and economics education, the Economics Education Society has created a wide range of free educational resources spanning macroeconomics, microeconomics and financial literacy. Chapters will receive teaching guides, lesson materials, as well as additional practice problems to create a foundational curriculum. We hope that members can use this information to not only gain essential knowledge regarding economics, but to also pursue valuable opportunities in the future.

Macroeconomics

Topics from GDP and CPI to whether or not a country should choose to trade.

Microeconomics

Covers fields from the different types of businesses to picking the best combinations of purchases.

Financial Literacy

Shows people the right decisions to make with personal finances and helps one in filing taxes and other important life-long skills.
